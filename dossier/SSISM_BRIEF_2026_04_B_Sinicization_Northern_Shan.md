## 🧠 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

As of April 2026, the operational trajectory of the former "Operation 1027" coalition has transitioned from military coordination into localized administrative consolidation under Chinese-aligned economic and governance systems.

The Myanmar National Democratic Alliance Army (MNDAA) has established a de facto administrative order in parts of Northern Shan State, characterized by increasing institutional alignment with Chinese linguistic, financial, and infrastructural systems.

Concurrently, the Myanmar military regime (SAC) has adopted limited integration of the Chinese Yuan (CNY) within official investment mechanisms, reflecting structural financial pressure and external dependency realignment.

This briefing examines these developments through an OSINT-based structural lens.
