## 1. What the NLD Officially Said (May 3)

On May 3, the National League for Democracy (NLD) issued a central committee statement that contains three critical elements:

1. **Acknowledgement and thanks** to China for stating it is monitoring Daw Aung San Suu Kyi’s situation and describing her as a “long-time friend”.
2. **Rejection** of the military’s move from prison to house arrest as a *political deception*.
3. **Warning** to ASEAN and the international community **not to be misled** by gestures that could grant legitimacy to Min Aung Hlaing’s planned sham election.

The NLD position is very precise:

> Symbolic gestures are **not** political solutions.  
> Legitimacy must not be granted.

This is a **legitimacy alert**, not a diplomatic message.

- Myanmar’s crisis is driven by **decades of impunity**
- The world must cut the junta’s **three lifelines**:
  - Money
  - Weapons & aviation fuel
